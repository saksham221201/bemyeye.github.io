Our Vision

Vision plays a vital role at every moment of our life. Humans use vision to navigate through he world and compute the observation. However blind people are not blessed with this gift, that makes their life quite difficult. The statics of WHO shows that 2.2 Billion people are diagnosed with either of the type of visual impairment. Also a report by journal states that this number is going to be threefold by the end of 2050.

Our Mission

The aim of this project is to help visually impaired people to get the information of the products by audio description, in just one step process i.e. by scanning the specially designed QR on the product.
